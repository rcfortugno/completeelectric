About Us

Founded in 1984, Complete Electric Motor Repair Ltd. has been providing expert electric motor repair services to Calgary and surrounding areas. Our mission is to deliver high-quality, efficient, and cost-effective solutions to keep your motors running smoothly.

Our Services

  • Electric Motor Rewinding: Extend the life of your motors with our precise rewinding services.
  • Troubleshooting and Diagnostics: Minimize downtime with accurate diagnostics.
  • Preventative Maintenance: Ensure reliability and efficiency with regular maintenance.
  • Motor Replacement: Upgrade to energy-efficient systems with our seamless motor replacement services.
