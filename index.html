About Us

Welcome to Complete Electric Motor Repair Ltd. Established in 1986, we are a family-owned and operated business proudly serving Calgary and the surrounding areas. Our commitment to quality and exceptional service has made us a trusted partner for businesses and individuals alike.

At Complete Electric Motor Repair Ltd., we specialize in repairing electric motors of all kinds. From pumps and fans to heavy-duty industrial motors, our skilled technicians ensure the job is done right. With over three decades of experience, we are dedicated to delivering reliable, efficient, and timely repairs.

Why Choose Us?

  • Over 30 years of trusted service in Calgary.
  • Experienced technicians using advanced tools and techniques.
  • Family-owned and operated, ensuring personalized care.
  • Comprehensive services for all types of electric motors.
