About Us

Welcome to Complete Electric Motor Repair Ltd. Established in 1986, we are a family-owned and operated business proudly serving Calgary and the surrounding areas. Our commitment to quality and exceptional service has made us a trusted partner for businesses and individuals alike.

At Complete Electric Motor Repair Ltd., we specialize in repairing electric motors of all kinds. From pumps and fans to heavy-duty industrial motors, our skilled technicians ensure the job is done right.
